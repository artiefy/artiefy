# Artiefy Estudiantes Silent Reel Design

## Visual Identity

This reel is based specifically on `https://artiefy.com`. The page is a dark educational discovery interface with cyan highlights, course cards, filters, program lists and a final course grid. The video should feel like a quiet cinematic screen recording: slow camera movement, clean overlays, subtle zooms and no audio of any kind.

## Palette

- **Deep Navy** `#01142B`: main background.
- **Header Navy** `#01152D`: navigation and browser chrome.
- **Card Navy** `#061C37`: course card surface.
- **Panel Navy** `#1D283A`: elevated panels and overlays.
- **Artiefy Cyan** `#22C4D3`: primary highlight and focus ring.
- **Electric Cyan** `#00D3F2`: scan lines and small markers.
- **Teal Glow** `#00BBA7`: atmospheric glow.
- **White** `#FFFFFF`: headlines.
- **Soft White** `#F8FAFC`: course card titles.
- **Muted Slate** `#94A3B8`: metadata.

## Typography

- Use **Montserrat** from captured font files.
- Captured font: `capture/assets/fonts/2b402d8aebffd579-s.0dskwooysu3cf.woff2`.
- Overlay headlines: 58-78px, 850-900 weight.
- Labels: 22-28px, uppercase, letter spacing `0.16em`.
- Course names in composed cards: 28-34px, 800 weight.

## Video Rules

1. No audio tags, no music, no narration and no SFX.
2. Show the real captured `/estudiantes` page from top to bottom.
3. Keep web screenshots as the primary content, not generic illustrations.
4. Use course cards and captured course images from the page for spotlight moments.
5. Motion must be slow, cinematic and readable.
6. Text overlays must be short and not block the course cards.
